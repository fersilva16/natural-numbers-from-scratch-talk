# What are natural numbers

<ul>
  <Appear>
    <li>Set of numbers</li>
    <li>Used to count: 0, 1, 2, 3...</li>
    <li>Any non-negative integer</li>
    <li>Represented by the double-struck capital N symbol (ℕ)</li>
  </Appear>
</ul>

---

# What are Peano numbers

<ul>
  <Appear>
    <li>Simple way to represent natural numbers</li>
    <li>Zero value and successor function</li>
    <li>Presented by Giuseppe Peano in the 19th century</li>
  </Appear>
</ul>
